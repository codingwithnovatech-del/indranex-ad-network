Account Approval Process

Last updated: June 24, 2026

Understanding the account approval process helps you prepare a successful application. Indranex AdNet reviews every publisher account carefully to maintain the quality of our network. Below is the complete step-by-step process from registration to final approval or rejection.

Step 1: Registration (Automatic)

When you complete the registration form on our website, your account is created automatically. You will receive a confirmation email with a link to verify your email address. This step is instant and does not require manual review. Ensure you use a valid email address that you check regularly all network communications are sent to this address.

Step 2: Email Verification

Click the verification link sent to your email inbox. If you do not see the email within 5 minutes, check your spam or junk folder. You must verify your email within 48 hours; unverified accounts are automatically deleted. After successful verification, you may log in and submit your website for review.

Step 3: Site Review

Our content quality team manually reviews your website. The review examines the following:

  • Domain Check: We verify domain ownership and confirm the domain is not blacklisted or associated with spam.
  • Content Audit: We evaluate content originality, depth, and relevance. Sites with thin, scraped, or AI-generated content without editorial oversight are rejected.
  • Quality Assessment: We assess site design, navigation, page load speed, mobile responsiveness, and overall user experience. Professional-looking sites with proper structure receive faster approvals.
  • Policy Compliance: We check for prohibited content categories as outlined in our Publisher Requirements policy.

Step 4: Ad Integration Verification

Once your site passes content review, you will be asked to integrate our ad code and verify domain ownership. After integration, our system serves a test ad to confirm the code is working correctly. We verify that ad containers render properly across desktop and mobile viewports and that no existing ad placements violate our spacing or stacking rules.

Step 5: Approval or Rejection Notification

You will receive a notification via email and in your dashboard within 24 to 48 hours of submitting your site for review. If approved, your account is activated immediately and ads will begin serving on your site within minutes. If rejected, the notification will include specific reasons for the decision and guidance on what to improve.

Common Rejection Reasons and How to Fix Them

  • Insufficient Content: Your site has fewer than 10 pages or posts. Publish more original, high-quality content before reapplying.
  • Poor Design or Usability: Your site is difficult to navigate, has broken links, or loads slowly. Improve site structure and optimize page speed.
  • Prohibited Content: Your site contains material that violates our content policy. Remove offending content and reapply.
  • Missing Privacy Policy: Your site lacks a privacy policy page. Create a comprehensive policy that covers data collection and third-party cookies.
  • Domain Age: Your domain is less than 30 days old. Wait until the domain matures and reapply.

Re-Application Process

If your account was rejected, you may address the issues listed in the rejection notice and submit a new application after 30 days. Submit a support ticket through your dashboard to request re-review. Ensure all previous rejection reasons have been fully resolved before reapplying. Repeated applications without meaningful changes may result in permanent disqualification.
