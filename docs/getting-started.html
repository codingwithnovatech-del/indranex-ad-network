Getting Started as a Publisher

Last updated: June 24, 2026

Welcome to Indranex AdNet, the premium advertising network built for publishers who value quality, transparency, and performance. This guide walks you through everything you need to start monetizing your website traffic in under 15 minutes.

Step 1: Create Your Account

Visit the Indranex AdNet homepage and click "Sign Up." You will need a valid email address, your full name, and your website URL. Choose a strong password and agree to our Publisher Terms. After submission, check your inbox for a verification email and click the confirmation link to activate your account.

Step 2: Register Your Site

Once logged in, navigate to the "Sites" section and click "Add New Site." Enter your domain name (e.g., example.com) and select your primary content category. Our system will generate a unique Site ID for each registered domain. You must verify ownership of the domain before ads will serve.

Step 3: Integrate the Ad Script

After verification, obtain your integration code from the dashboard. The code consists of a base script tag followed by ad slot placeholders. Place the base script once in your site's <head> section. For each ad slot, use the data-position attribute to define placement type:

Example: Available data-position values include "top-banner," "sidebar," "in-article," "below-content," "popunder," "interstitial," and "social-bar."

The ad script automatically renders the most relevant ad format for each position. Ensure your page layout accommodates ad containers without breaking responsiveness.

Step 4: Verify Domain Ownership

We support three verification methods: adding a DNS TXT record to your domain, uploading an HTML verification file to your server root, or adding a meta tag to your homepage. Choose the method most convenient for you. Verification typically completes within minutes.

Step 5: Configure Ad Placements

Decide where ads should appear on your pages. We recommend placing ad slots in high-visibility areas while maintaining a clean user experience. Use A/B testing to find the optimal balance between revenue and user engagement. Remember that you may place a maximum of three ad slots per page.

Step 6: Monitor Your Earnings

Your dashboard provides real-time analytics including impressions, clicks, estimated revenue, fill rate, and eCPM. Track performance daily and experiment with different placement configurations to maximize your earnings.

Troubleshooting FAQ

Q: Ads are not showing on my site.
A: Verify the script is correctly placed in the <head> section, domain ownership is verified, and your account is approved. Also check for JavaScript errors in your browser console.

Q: Why is my eCPM lower than expected?
A: eCPM varies by geography, niche, traffic quality, and ad placement. Ensure you have quality content and organic traffic sources.

Q: Can I use Indranex AdNet alongside other ad networks?
A: Yes, as long as you do not exceed three ad slots per page and comply with our ad stacking policies.
