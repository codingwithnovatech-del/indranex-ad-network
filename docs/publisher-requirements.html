Publisher Requirements & Eligibility

Last updated: June 24, 2026

To maintain a high-quality advertising ecosystem, Indranex AdNet enforces strict eligibility criteria for all publishers. Please review the following requirements carefully before submitting your application. Meeting these standards ensures a fair and profitable experience for advertisers, publishers, and users alike.

Minimum Requirements

Your website must meet the following baseline criteria to be considered for our network:

  • You must own or have full operational control over the website domain.
  • The site must contain original, valuable content spanning at least 10 published pages or posts.
  • Content must be written primarily for human visitors and provide genuine informational or entertainment value.
  • A clear, accessible privacy policy page must be present on your site, disclosing cookie usage and third-party data collection.
  • Your domain must be registered for at least 30 days prior to application.
  • The site must have a functional contact page or about page with verifiable information.

Prohibited Content

Websites containing any of the following types of content will be immediately rejected or suspended:

  • Illegal activity, pirated software, or content promoting unlawful behavior
  • Adult or pornographic material, explicit sexual content, or adult dating
  • Hate speech, racial discrimination, violence, or harassment
  • Copyrighted material used without authorization
  • Sale of weapons, drugs, or controlled substances
  • Gambling or casino-related content (unless explicitly permitted by local law)
  • Deceptive, misleading, or clickbait content designed solely to generate ad revenue
  • Auto-generated or AI-produced content that lacks human editorial oversight

Traffic Quality Standards

All traffic directed to your site must originate from legitimate sources. We monitor traffic patterns continuously and expect:

  • A majority of traffic coming from organic search, direct visits, social media, or legitimate referral sources.
  • Bounce rates and session durations consistent with genuine user engagement.
  • No use of bots, auto-refresh scripts, or traffic exchange programs.

Compliance with Third-Party Policies

If you use Google AdSense or other third-party ad networks alongside Indranex AdNet, you remain fully responsible for compliance with their respective policies. Violations of Google AdSense policies may also affect your status with our network. We recommend reviewing and adhering to Google's Webmaster Quality Guidelines at all times.

Note: Indranex AdNet reserves the right to reject or remove any publisher at our sole discretion if the website or its traffic does not align with our quality standards. There is no guarantee of acceptance.

Account Approval Criteria

Our review team evaluates each application based on content quality, site design and usability, traffic legitimacy, and overall user experience. Sites with clean layouts, fast loading speeds, and well-structured content have a significantly higher approval rate. We also consider the niche category some verticals such as finance, health, and technology typically perform well on our network.
