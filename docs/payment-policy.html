Payment Policy & Payout Schedule

Last updated: June 24, 2026

Indranex AdNet is committed to transparent and timely payments for all publishers. This section details how earnings are calculated, when payouts are made, and what you need to do to receive your funds.

Revenue Calculation

Publisher earnings are calculated on a CPM (Cost Per Mille) basis, meaning you earn revenue for every 1,000 ad impressions served on your site. The base CPM rate is estimated at $0.50, though actual CPM may vary based on factors including geographic location of your audience, content niche, seasonality, ad format, and overall traffic quality. Premium traffic from Tier-1 countries (US, UK, Canada, Australia) typically commands higher CPM rates.

Revenue Share Model

Under our standard agreement, publishers receive 70% of gross ad revenue generated from their placements. Indranex AdNet retains 30% to cover operational costs, advertiser acquisition, technology infrastructure, and support services. There are no hidden fees or deductions.

NET-30 Payment Cycle

All earnings are calculated on a monthly basis. The NET-30 cycle means that revenue earned in a given calendar month is calculated and made available for payout by the end of the following month. For example, earnings from January will be processed for payment by the end of February.

Minimum Payout Threshold

Publishers must accumulate a minimum balance of $50.00 before a payout is initiated. If your balance falls below this threshold at the end of a payment cycle, the balance rolls over to the next month until the minimum is met. There is no maximum payout limit.

Available Payment Methods

We offer the following payout options to suit your preference:

  • Bank Transfer (Wire): Direct deposit to your bank account. Processing takes 3-5 business days after the cycle ends. Available in most countries.
  • PayPal: Instant transfer to your PayPal account. A $1 processing fee applies for amounts under $500.
  • Crypto (USDT): Tether (USDT) on the TRC-20 or ERC-20 network. Your wallet address must be provided in your payment settings. Network fees apply.

First Payment Holding Period

For new publishers, the first payment is subject to a 45-day holding period from the date of the first ad impression. This safeguard allows us to verify traffic quality and ensure compliance before releasing funds. Subsequent payments follow the standard NET-30 schedule.

Tax Information Requirements

Depending on your country of residence, you may need to submit tax documentation. US publishers must complete a W-9 form. International publishers may need to submit a W-8BEN form for tax treaty considerations. You can upload these documents in your account settings under the Tax Information section.

Payment Disputes

If you believe there is an error in your payment calculation, you have a 14-day window from the payout date to file a dispute. Contact our support team with detailed information, including screenshots and relevant timestamps. Disputes submitted after 14 days will not be considered.
