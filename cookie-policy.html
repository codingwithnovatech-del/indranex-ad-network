Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Indranex AdNet ("we", "us", "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website. By using our platform, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently and to provide information to website owners. Cookies can be "persistent" (remaining on your device for a set period) or "session" (deleted when you close your browser).

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies and browser storage technologies for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: We use localStorage and sessionStorage to remember your login session and preferences, such as the "remember me" feature. These are essential for the platform to function correctly.
  • Advertising: Google AdSense uses cookies to serve personalized ads based on your browsing history and interests. These cookies help advertisers show you relevant ads and help us earn revenue to keep our services free.
  • Analytics: Firebase and Cloudflare may set cookies to collect anonymous usage data, helping us improve our platform's performance and user experience.

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third-party services that we use. These include Google AdSense (for ad personalization and serving), Firebase (for real-time database and authentication), and Cloudflare (for security and performance optimization). Each of these providers has its own cookie and privacy policies governing their use of data.

How to Control Cookies

You can manage or delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies, but doing so may affect the functionality of our platform. Here are links to manage cookies in popular browsers:

  • Google Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies and other site data
  • Mozilla Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions

Advertising Opt-Out

To opt out of personalized advertising from Google, visit Google Ads Settings. To opt out of interest-based advertising from multiple networks, visit the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page.
