About Indranex AdNet

Empowering publishers & advertisers with transparent, real-time ad technology

Indranex AdNet is a full-featured private ad network platform built for modern publishers and advertisers. Founded in 2026, we provide a complete ecosystem for buying and selling digital advertising across multiple formats including popunders, banners, interstitials, native ads, video ads, and social bars.

Our platform is built on Google Firebase for real-time data processing and Cloudflare for security, ensuring low-latency ad delivery, accurate analytics, and robust fraud protection. We serve ads through our proprietary JavaScript SDK that integrates seamlessly into any website with a single script tag.

Our Mission

To democratize ad monetization by providing publishers of all sizes with enterprise-grade ad technology, transparent analytics, and fair payouts — while giving advertisers precise targeting and measurable ROI.

Why Choose Indranex AdNet?

  • Real-time analytics — Live impression, click, and revenue data updated in real-time via Firebase
  • 6 ad formats — Popunder, banner, interstitial, native, video, and social bar — cover every placement
  • One-click integration — Copy a single script tag, paste it into your website, ads start serving instantly
  • Fraud protection — Cloudflare Turnstile CAPTCHA, session-based rate limiting, and domain verification
  • Multi-publisher support — Manage unlimited sites from one dashboard with per-site analytics
  • Transparent reporting — Every impression and click is tracked with full timestamp and source data

Our Commitment

We are committed to maintaining a fair, transparent, and secure advertising ecosystem. We continuously monitor traffic quality, update our fraud detection systems, and work closely with our partners to maximize revenue and campaign performance. Our platform is designed to comply with applicable data protection regulations including GDPR and CCPA principles, and we partner with Google AdSense to provide additional monetization opportunities for qualifying publishers.
