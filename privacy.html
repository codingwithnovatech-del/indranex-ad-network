Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect account information (name, email, website) during registration. We also collect ad impression and click data for analytics and payment purposes.

2. How We Use Information

Account information is used to manage your account and communicate with you. Analytics data is used to measure ad performance and calculate earnings/billing.

3. Data Storage

Data is stored securely on Firebase Realtime Database (Google Cloud Platform). We implement basic security measures including password hashing (SHA-256) and CAPTCHA protection.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Cloudflare Turnstile for bot protection. Turnstile may collect anonymized browser data for fraud detection. See Cloudflare Privacy Policy.

5. Cookies

We use local storage and session storage for authentication purposes. No third-party tracking cookies are used.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties except as required by law or with your explicit consent.

7. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, please contact the platform administrator.
