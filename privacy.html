Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026 · Effective: Immediately

Indranex AdNet ("we," "our," "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our ad network platform, including any associated websites, services, and APIs (collectively, the "Platform").

Trusted Partners
Google Auth
Firebase
Cloudflare

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you register as a publisher or advertiser, we collect your name, email address, website URL, and account preferences. If you sign in using Google Authentication, we receive your Google profile email and display name from Google's secure OAuth service.

1.2 Ad & Analytics Data

We collect data about ad impressions, clicks, and conversions to measure performance, calculate payments, and optimize delivery. This data includes timestamps, page URLs, ad positions, device type, browser information, and anonymized IP addresses. This data is stored in Firebase Realtime Database and is never shared with unauthorized third parties.

1.3 Automated Data

We use Cloudflare Turnstile on our login and registration pages to prevent automated abuse. Turnstile may collect anonymized browsing data (including browser fingerprints, headers, and interaction patterns) to distinguish humans from bots. This data is governed by Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

1.4 Communications

If you contact us via email or support forms, we retain your messages and contact information to respond to your inquiries.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the Platform and your user experience
  • To process ad serving, track impressions/clicks, and calculate publisher earnings and advertiser bills
  • To communicate with you about your account, payments, platform updates, and policy changes
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and violations of our Terms of Service
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our rights

3. Data Storage & Security

3.1 Your data is stored on Firebase Realtime Database, which is hosted on Google Cloud Platform infrastructure (located in the United States and Europe). Firebase employs industry-standard physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards including encryption at rest and in transit.

3.2 Authentication is handled entirely by Firebase Authentication (Google). We never store or have access to your password. Google manages password hashing, rate limiting, and breach detection on our behalf.

3.3 We implement additional security measures including Cloudflare Turnstile CAPTCHA, rate limiting on login attempts (5 failures = 15 minute lockout), and session-based authentication tokens stored in localStorage/sessionStorage.

While we take reasonable measures to protect your data, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that process your data:

  • Google Firebase — Authentication, real-time database, and hosting. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Cloudflare — Turnstile CAPTCHA and DNS. Cloudflare Privacy Policy
  • Google OAuth — Optional Google Sign-In authentication. Google Privacy Policy
  • GitHub Pages — Platform hosting. GitHub Privacy Statement

5. Cookies & Local Storage

5.1 We use browser localStorage and sessionStorage strictly for authentication purposes. When you select "Remember Me," a timestamped token is stored in localStorage for 24 hours. Without this option, a session-only token is stored in sessionStorage and cleared when you close your browser.

5.2 We do not use cookies for tracking, advertising, or analytics. We do not employ third-party tracking cookies, pixel tags, or fingerprinting scripts beyond Cloudflare Turnstile's anonymized bot detection.

5.3 You can clear this data at any time via your browser settings. Note that clearing it will log you out of the Platform.

6. Data Sharing & Disclosure

6.1 We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

6.2 We may share aggregated, anonymized data (e.g., total impressions, average CTR) with partners or in public materials for marketing purposes. This data cannot identify individual users.

6.3 We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government regulation, or if we believe in good faith that such disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.

6.4 In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred to the acquiring entity. You will be notified via email and/or a prominent notice on our Platform.

7. Data Retention

7.1 We retain your account information for as long as your account is active and for a reasonable period thereafter to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

7.2 Analytics data (impressions, clicks) may be retained indefinitely in aggregated form for reporting purposes. Individual-level analytics records older than 24 months may be automatically purged.

7.3 Upon account deletion, we will remove or anonymize your personal information within 30 days, except where retention is legally required.

8. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Portability: Request a machine-readable copy of your data
  • Objection: Object to our processing of your data for legitimate interests

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@indranexadnet.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

The Platform is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal data, we will delete it promptly. If you believe a child has submitted data to us, please contact us immediately.

10. International Users

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own, including India and the United States. By using the Platform, you consent to such transfer. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

11. Changes to This Policy

11.1 We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be announced via email or a prominent notice on the Platform at least 14 days before taking effect.

11.2 Your continued use of the Platform after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy. We encourage you to review this page regularly.
