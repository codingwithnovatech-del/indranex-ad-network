Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Indranex AdNet — from joining to earning

What is Indranex AdNet? ▼
Indranex AdNet is a premium private ad network that connects publishers with advertisers. We offer 6 ad formats including popunders, banners, interstitials, native ads, video ads, and social bar ads. Publishers earn 70% revenue share with real-time analytics, instant integration, and reliable NET-30 payouts.
How do I join Indranex AdNet as a publisher? ▼
Visit our homepage and click "Start Earning" to create your free account. You'll need a valid email address and your website URL. After email verification, register your site in the publisher dashboard, copy the integration code, paste it into your website, and start earning immediately.
How long does account approval take? ▼
Account registration is automatic and instant. Site review and approval typically take 24-48 hours. During this period, our team verifies your domain, checks content quality, and ensures compliance with our policies. You can integrate the ad code immediately — ads will start serving once approved.
What websites are accepted? ▼
We accept websites with original content, a clear privacy policy, and legitimate traffic. Sites with illegal content, hate speech, adult material, or auto-generated spam content are not accepted. Each site is manually reviewed before full approval is granted.
What traffic sources are allowed? ▼
We accept organic traffic from search engines, direct traffic, social media referrals, and legitimate email campaigns. Prohibited sources include bots, click farms, traffic exchange programs, auto-refresh traffic, incentivized clicks, and any form of artificial traffic manipulation. All traffic must be from real human users.
What is the minimum payout? ▼
The minimum payout threshold is $50 for all payment methods. If your earnings do not reach $50 in a month, the balance rolls over to the next month until the threshold is met. There are no fees associated with payouts.
How are earnings calculated? ▼
Revenue is calculated on a CPM (Cost Per Mille) basis. You earn for every 1,000 verified impressions served on your site. The CPM rate varies based on your traffic geography, niche, ad format, and seasonal demand. Publishers receive 70% of the gross revenue. Your dashboard shows real-time earnings with detailed breakdowns.
Which countries are supported? ▼
We accept publishers from all countries. However, advertiser demand varies by geography. Traffic from tier-1 countries (US, UK, Canada, Australia, Western Europe) generally earns higher CPM rates. Indian traffic earns competitive rates based on current market demand. Our system automatically matches the best available campaigns.
How do payments work? ▼
Payments are processed on a NET-30 cycle. Earnings from each calendar month are calculated on the 1st of the following month and paid out within 30 days. Your first payment may have a 45-day holding period for fraud verification. Payments are sent via your chosen method — bank transfer (NEFT/IMPS), PayPal, or cryptocurrency (USDT).
What ad formats are available? ▼
We support 6 ad formats: (1) Banner — image or text display ads, (2) Popunder — opens in new window behind current, (3) Interstitial — full-screen overlay with timer, (4) Native — in-content ads matching site style, (5) Video — YouTube or MP4 pre-rolls, (6) Social Bar — sticky bottom bar with call-to-action. Each format can be enabled or disabled per campaign.
Can I use multiple ad formats on the same page? ▼
Yes, you can use multiple ad formats on the same page. We recommend a maximum of 3 ad slots per page to maintain good user experience. Popunders and interstitials are special formats that serve once per session regardless of how many slots you place.
What happens if I violate the traffic policy? ▼
Traffic policy violations are taken seriously. Depending on severity, consequences include: a warning with corrective action, payment hold for investigation, temporary account suspension, or permanent account termination with forfeiture of outstanding balance. We recommend reading our Traffic Policy and Invalid Click Policy carefully.
Do I need a privacy policy on my website? ▼
Yes, all publishers must have a privacy policy on their website that discloses the use of third-party advertising and cookies. This is required by Google AdSense policies and data protection regulations. Your privacy policy should inform visitors about cookies used for personalized advertising.
Can I use Indranex AdNet alongside Google AdSense? ▼
Yes, you can use Indranex AdNet alongside Google AdSense and other ad networks. Our ad SDK is designed to coexist with other ad scripts without conflicts. However, ensure you do not exceed recommended ad density limits and that total ad placements comply with all applicable policies.
How do I integrate the ad code? ▼
Integration is simple: Log into your publisher dashboard, go to the Integration tab, copy the generated code, and paste it into your website's HTML. The code consists of ad slot divs and a script tag. Place ad slot divs at desired positions and the script tag anywhere on the page. Ads will start serving automatically.
How do I track my earnings? ▼
Your publisher dashboard provides real-time earnings tracking including total earnings, daily breakdown (7-day chart), earnings by ad format, estimated vs confirmed earnings, withdrawal history, and pending payouts. All data updates instantly as impressions and clicks are recorded.
Can I add multiple websites? ▼
Yes, you can register multiple websites under a single publisher account. Each site gets a unique Site ID, separate integration code, and per-site analytics. This allows you to manage all your properties from one dashboard while tracking performance independently.
What if my site gets rejected? ▼
If your site is rejected, you will receive an email with specific reasons. Common reasons include insufficient original content, prohibited content categories, broken navigation, missing privacy policy, or domain verification failure. Fix the issues and re-apply by contacting our support team.
Is there a mobile app for the dashboard? ▼
Currently we do not have a dedicated mobile app. However, our dashboard is fully responsive and works seamlessly on mobile browsers. You can log in, check earnings, view stats, and manage settings — all optimized for touch interaction on any device.
How does the popunder format work? ▼
The popunder format opens the advertiser's URL in a new browser window behind the current window. It is triggered by the user's first click anywhere on the page, ensuring it only fires with genuine user interaction. The popunder is counted once per session and typically has the highest CPM rates.
What is the revenue share percentage? ▼
Publishers receive 70% of the gross ad revenue generated from their traffic. This is one of the highest revenue shares in the industry — most competing networks offer only 50-60%. We believe in fair partnerships with no hidden deductions.
How do I contact support? ▼
You can reach our support team via email at support@indranexadnet.com or through our contact form. We aim to respond to all inquiries within 48 hours. For faster assistance, include your Site ID in the subject line.
